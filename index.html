About the Academy

Nurturing Mandya's Tennis Talents Under Elite Mentorship

Top Serve Tennis Academy has established itself as the leading sports coaching institution in Mandya, Karnataka. Nestled within the prestigious P.E.T Tennis Stadium at the PES Engineering College campus, our academy offers an inspiring environment where discipline, passion, and high performance intersect.

Under the guidance of Manjunath M S (Head of the Academy and Veteran Coach), we design tailored progression pathways for every player. Whether you are an absolute beginner picking up a racket for the first time, a corporate professional staying fit, or a competitive junior athlete training for national tournaments, our structured methodology ensures steady improvement.

Unnathi Veda Muralidhar

Winner, Open Women's Singles Championship

Unnathi Veda Muralidhar is a prominent 15-year-old tennis prospect from Karnataka, currently training out of Mandya, who has achieved several notable milestones on the All India Tennis Association (AITA).

In the women's singles semifinal events, Unnathi fought back after dropping the opening set to defeat Sonicka 1-6, 6-4, 10-7 to set up a summit clash with Nidi S, a first-time contestant, who beat defending champion Trisha Vinod in the other semifinal. Unnathi went on to capture the championship, bringing home double trophies.
